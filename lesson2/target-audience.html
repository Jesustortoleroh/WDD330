Audience

Outdoor enthusiasts of all kinds, including paddlers, surfers, thrill seekers, adventurers, hikers, bicyclists, nature lovers, birders, and others interested in enjoying the benefits of outdoor recreation, for workers and students, for adults and children. For People who live in Valencia, Venezuela or around the world, those who do adventure tourism . Most of our visitors will access it from their mobile device.

Two personas

Jhon Williams

Jhon is a 17-year-old young man who likes sports and outdoor activities. He is thinking of going to the beach with his friends, he wants to meet them and have a good time. Some people have told him that he should be careful in cyclone and hurricane season. He wants to know what will be the best time to enjoy the waves without posing any danger and that is why he will use his mobile device to find out.

Silvia Davis

Silvia is 42 years old and likes to enjoy the country air. She wants to travel with her family and enjoy the scenery. She enjoys riding horses and feeding animals. She will check with her mobile phone if there is rain, since the streets can be swampy and she wants to be sure of not having any setbacks.
