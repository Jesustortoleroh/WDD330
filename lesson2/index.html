Weather Site Name

The name of the site is "Dream Weather".

Site Purpose

The purpose of Dream Weather is to predict in real time, the state of the atmosphere for a future period. Weather forecasts for thousands of locations around the world. People who enter our site can access data about the state of the atmosphere (temperature, atmospheric pressure, winds, humidity and precipitation) and atmospheric processes (through meteorology) to determine future atmospheric patterns. They can also access GPS maps and comparative weather reports. For us, the prevention is our priority. We promote environmentalism and sustainable development
